Featured Project

HSE Incident Reporting &
Safety Knowledge Hub

Developed a custom HSE application for near-miss and hazardous activity reporting,
integrating Job Safety Reports to significantly reduce workplace incidents across regions.

The Problem

The organisation faced significant challenges in health, safety, and environment (HSE) management across multiple sites and regions:

  • No centralised system for incident reporting and tracking
  • Near-misses and hazardous activities often went unreported
  • Inconsistent safety approval processes across sites
  • No visibility into incident trends or root causes
  • Safety learnings not shared across regions
  • Workplace incidents impacting employee safety and operational continuity
  • Manual paper-based Job Safety Reports (JSR) causing delays and missed approvals

My Role

I led the design, development, and implementation of the HSE application. My responsibilities included:

  • Requirements Gathering: Interviewed HSE officers, site managers, and field staff to understand needs
  • Application Design: Designed custom ServiceNow application for incident reporting
  • Workflow Development: Built automated approval workflows for Job Safety Reports
  • Mobile Interface: Ensured mobile accessibility for field teams
  • Knowledge Hub: Created centralised repository for safety learnings
  • Training & Rollout: Trained staff across all regions on the new system

Approach & Methodology

Step 1: Requirements Gathering

Conducted workshops with HSE stakeholders to understand requirements:

  • Field Staff: Needed simple, mobile-friendly reporting interface
  • HSE Officers: Required investigation workflows and trend analysis
  • Leadership: Wanted visibility into safety performance and incident trends
  • Compliance Team: Needed audit trails and regulatory reporting capabilities

Step 2: Incident Types Defined

Defined categories for different incident types:

Near-Miss An incident that could have resulted in injury or damage but did not
Hazardous Activity Unsafe acts or conditions observed that could lead to incidents
Incident Actual events causing injury, illness, or damage
Slip Hazard Specific hazard category for slips, trips, and falls
Job Safety Report (JSR) Pre-activity safety assessment requiring approval before work begins

Step 3: Application Development

Built custom ServiceNow application with key features:

  • Incident Reporting Form: Mobile-friendly form with guided fields for incident capture
  • Photo Upload: Ability to attach photos of hazards or incidents
  • Location Tracking: Automatic capture of site and region information
  • Severity Assessment: Automated severity calculation based on incident type and impact
  • Investigation Workflow: Structured process for root cause analysis and corrective actions

Step 4: Job Safety Report (JSR) Workflow

Implemented automated JSR approval workflow:

  • Field staff submit JSR before starting high-risk activities
  • Automatic routing to appropriate approver based on activity type and site
  • Escalation if approval not received within specified timeframe
  • Work cannot proceed until JSR is approved
  • Completed JSRs archived for audit and reference

Step 5: Safety Knowledge Hub

Created centralised knowledge repository:

  • Incident Database: Searchable archive of all reported incidents
  • Root Cause Library: Common root causes with preventive measures
  • Corrective Action Tracker: Track implementation of corrective actions
  • Safety Alerts: Push notifications for emerging safety issues
  • Best Practices: Documented safety practices by activity type
  • Regional Dashboards: Compare safety performance across regions

Step 6: Analytics & Reporting

Developed Power BI dashboards for safety analytics:

  • Incident trends over time by region and type
  • Near-miss to incident conversion rates
  • JSR approval times and compliance rates
  • Corrective action completion status
  • Safety performance scorecards for each site
Technologies Used:
ServiceNow Custom App Flow Designer Mobile Interface Power BI SQL

Challenges Overcome

  • User Adoption: Field staff initially resistant to additional reporting. Addressed through mobile-friendly design and demonstrating value of near-miss reporting for prevention.
  • Data Quality: Ensured consistent incident categorisation through guided forms and validation rules.
  • Integration with Existing Systems: Integrated with HR system for employee records and site master data.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensured system met all regulatory reporting requirements for HSE incidents.
  • Real-time Alerts: Configured automated alerts for critical incidents requiring immediate attention.

Outcome & Impact

  • Significant reduction in workplace incidents through proactive hazard identification
  • Near-miss reporting increased by 200% enabling preventive actions
  • 100% JSR compliance achieved across all high-risk activities
  • Centralised safety knowledge hub accessible to all regions
  • Faster incident investigation through structured workflows (7 days → 3 days average)
  • Improved safety culture with proactive hazard reporting encouraged
  • Audit-ready reporting for regulatory compliance

Key Learnings

This project taught me that safety systems need to be easy to use, or people won't use them. The mobile-first design and simple reporting interface were critical to adoption. When reporting is frictionless, people report more—and more reporting enables better prevention.

I also learned that near-miss reporting is the most valuable safety metric. Every near-miss is a free lesson about how to prevent future incidents. Creating a culture where near-misses are reported without fear of blame was essential.
