Featured Project

EV Station Lifecycle Management
& Go-Live Optimization

Managed end-to-end lifecycle for 400+ EV charging points,
achieving 99.5% operational uptime and reducing go-live time by 20%.

The Problem

The organisation was rapidly scaling its EV charging network from 100 to 400+ stations within 24 months. This rapid growth created significant operational challenges:

  • Inconsistent onboarding processes across different regions
  • Compliance verification delays causing go-live bottlenecks
  • No centralised tracking of station readiness status
  • Maintenance coordination was reactive rather than proactive
  • Issue resolution varied by region with no standard framework
  • Go-live timelines inconsistent, ranging from 30 to 90 days per station
  • Operational downtime affecting revenue and customer satisfaction

My Role

I managed the end-to-end EV station lifecycle and led the optimisation efforts. My responsibilities included:

  • Lifecycle Management: Oversaw station onboarding, compliance, maintenance, and commissioning for 400+ points
  • Process Optimisation: Identified bottlenecks and implemented streamlined workflows
  • Dashboard Development: Built tracking dashboards for clearance status and go-live readiness
  • Issue Resolution: Developed structured framework for incident management
  • Knowledge Management: Created knowledge base of recurring issues
  • Cross-functional Coordination: Bridged commercial, technical, and regulatory teams

Approach & Methodology

Step 1: Process Mapping

Mapped the complete station lifecycle from initial onboarding to successful go-live:

Stage 1: Onboarding
Site identification, land acquisition, legal agreements, feasibility study
Stage 2: Document Clearance
Permits, regulatory approvals, grid connection agreements, environmental clearances
Stage 3: Compliance Verification
Safety audits, electrical compliance, fire safety, accessibility standards
Stage 4: Infrastructure Setup
Equipment installation, software configuration, network connectivity, testing
Stage 5: Commissioning
Final inspections, system integration, user acceptance testing, go-live approval

Step 2: Dashboard Development

Created real-time dashboards to track station readiness:

  • Clearance Status Dashboard: Tracked document submission, approval status, and pending items by station
  • Vendor Documentation Tracker: Monitored vendor compliance and documentation completion
  • Go-Live Readiness Dashboard: Provided real-time visibility into station readiness across all stages
  • Regional Performance Dashboard: Compared go-live times and compliance rates across regions

Step 3: Bottleneck Identification & Resolution

Identified key bottlenecks through data analysis:

  • Document clearance delays: Average 15 days lost due to missing permits
  • Compliance verification: Inconsistent standards across regions causing rework
  • Vendor coordination: Delays in equipment delivery and installation

Implemented solutions including standardised checklists, automated reminders, and dedicated vendor coordinators.

Step 4: Issue Resolution Framework

Implemented structured issue resolution framework:

  • Severity Categorisation: Classified incidents as Critical, High, Medium, Low based on business impact
  • MTTR Tracking: Monitored Mean Time to Repair for each severity level
  • Escalation Matrix: Defined escalation paths based on severity and duration
  • Root Cause Analysis: Conducted RCA for recurring incidents

Step 5: Knowledge Base Development

Built a centralised knowledge base of recurring issues and solutions:

  • Documented common issues and resolution steps
  • Categorised by station type, region, and component
  • Made accessible to field teams via mobile interface
  • Regularly updated based on new incident patterns

Step 6: Scaling Support

Developed scalable processes to support rapid growth:

  • Standardised onboarding checklists for all new stations
  • Automated compliance verification workflows
  • Created vendor performance scorecards
  • Implemented weekly governance reviews for at-risk stations

Challenges Overcome

  • Rapid Scaling: Grew from 100 to 400+ stations in 24 months while maintaining quality and compliance.
  • Regional Inconsistency: Different regions had different processes. Standardised without losing local flexibility.
  • Vendor Coordination: Managed 50+ vendors across multiple regions with varying performance levels.
  • Regulatory Complexity: Navigated different regulatory requirements across jurisdictions.
  • Field Team Adoption: Ensured field teams adopted new processes through training and support.

Outcome & Impact

  • 99.5% operational uptime maintained across 400+ stations
  • Go-live time reduced by 20% from 60-90 days to 48-72 days average
  • Repeat incidents reduced by 25% through knowledge base implementation
  • 100% compliance verification achieved for all stations
  • Standardised processes implemented across all regions
  • Field teams resolved common issues independently reducing escalation time by 40%

Key Learnings

This project taught me that scalability requires intentional process design. What works for 100 stations breaks at 400. Building standardised, repeatable processes early made the scaling journey much smoother.

I also learned that visibility is a force multiplier. Simple dashboards showing clearance status and go-live readiness helped everyone—from field teams to leadership—understand where bottlenecks existed and take action.

Finally, investing in a knowledge base paid off enormously. Field teams solving common issues independently freed up escalation teams to focus on complex problems.
