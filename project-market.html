The Problem

The organisation was participating in flexibility service markets but faced significant challenges in maximising revenue and optimising portfolio performance:

  • No centralised system for preparing station portfolios for market auctions
  • Manual pre-qualification documentation causing delays and missed opportunities
  • Limited visibility into asset availability across Day-ahead, Intraday, and Balancing Mechanism markets
  • Inability to track bidding performance and revenue trends
  • Reactive maintenance causing asset unavailability during peak market opportunities
  • No data-driven approach to optimise bidding strategies

My Role

I supported the end-to-end market auction participation and portfolio performance management. My responsibilities included:

  • Portfolio Preparation: Prepared station portfolios for market auctions across multiple market types
  • Documentation Management: Managed pre-qualification documentation and compliance requirements
  • Asset Tracking: Tracked asset availability and readiness for market participation
  • Dashboard Development: Built Power BI dashboards for bidding performance and revenue tracking
  • Predictive Analytics: Applied Python to forecast station utilisation during peak demand
  • Performance Analysis: Analysed bidding performance to identify improvement opportunities

Approach & Methodology

Step 1: Market Understanding

Developed deep understanding of different flexibility service markets:

📅 Day-ahead Market
Auctions held one day before delivery. Requires accurate forecasting of available capacity and price bidding.
⚡ Intraday Market
Continuous trading on the delivery day. Requires real-time visibility into asset availability and rapid response.
⚖️ Balancing Mechanism
Real-time market for system balancing. Requires high-availability assets and automated response capabilities.

Step 2: Portfolio Preparation Process

Established standardised process for portfolio preparation:

  • Asset Inventory: Maintained up-to-date inventory of all 400+ charging points with technical specifications
  • Availability Tracking: Monitored real-time asset availability based on maintenance schedules and operational status
  • Capacity Forecasting: Used historical data to forecast available capacity for each market
  • Documentation Management: Centralised all pre-qualification documents required for market participation

Step 3: Pre-qualification Management

Streamlined pre-qualification process:

  • Created centralised repository for all pre-qualification documents
  • Implemented automated reminders for document renewal and expiry
  • Standardised documentation format across all stations
  • Reduced pre-qualification time from 30 to 15 days

Step 4: Dashboard Development

Created comprehensive Power BI dashboards for market performance tracking:

  • Portfolio Utilisation Dashboard: Real-time view of asset utilisation across markets
  • Bidding Performance Dashboard: Success rates, revenue trends, and bid optimisation insights
  • Revenue Tracking Dashboard: Revenue by market type, station, and time period
  • Availability Dashboard: Asset availability forecast for upcoming market opportunities

Step 5: Predictive Analytics Implementation

Applied Python predictive analytics to improve market participation:

  • Forecast station utilisation during peak demand periods
  • Predict available capacity for Day-ahead and Intraday markets
  • Identify optimal bidding windows based on historical patterns
  • Enable proactive maintenance scheduling to maximise availability during high-value periods

Step 6: Performance Optimisation

Continuously optimised market participation based on performance data:

  • Analysed bidding success rates to refine bidding strategies
  • Identified underperforming stations for targeted improvements
  • Optimised portfolio composition based on market performance
  • Developed recommendations for new market opportunities
Technologies Used:
Power BI SQL Python (pandas, scikit-learn) Predictive Analytics Excel

Challenges Overcome

  • Market Complexity: Three different market types with different rules, timelines, and requirements. Developed standardised approach while accommodating market-specific needs.
  • Data Integration: Needed real-time data from multiple sources including operations, maintenance, and market systems.
  • Forecast Accuracy: Achieving accurate utilisation forecasts required sophisticated predictive models and continuous refinement.
  • Documentation Burden: Managing pre-qualification documentation for 400+ stations was resource-intensive. Automation and centralisation significantly reduced effort.
  • Proactive Maintenance: Shifting from reactive to proactive maintenance required cultural change and new processes.

Outcome & Impact

  • Portfolio revenue increased by 15% through optimised market participation
  • Pre-qualification time reduced by 50% (30 days to 15 days)
  • Improved asset availability through predictive maintenance scheduling
  • Real-time visibility into portfolio performance across all markets
  • Data-driven bidding strategies replacing guesswork
  • Identified new revenue opportunities in emerging flexibility markets

Key Learnings

This project taught me that data-driven market participation can significantly impact revenue. Before this initiative, bidding decisions were based on intuition. With real-time dashboards and predictive analytics, we transformed market participation into a strategic advantage.

I also learned that asset availability is the foundation of market success. The most sophisticated bidding strategy fails if assets aren't available. Proactive maintenance scheduling based on market opportunities was a game-changer.
